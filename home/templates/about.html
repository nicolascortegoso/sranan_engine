A small collection of annotated sentences for Sranan Tongo

Users can find here sentences extracted from reliable sources for the study of Sranan Tongo. The main sources are the following:

  • Nickel Marilyn, Wilner John (1984) Papers on Sranan Tongo
  • Winford Donald, Plag Ingo (2013) Sranan structure dataset
  • Wilner John (2007) Wortubuku fu Sranan Tongo

A sentence goes through three different stages:

The first stage corresponds to the raw sentence without annotations with its corresponding English and/or Dutch translations and attribution to the author. Users can contribute to the project by annotating the sentences with part-of-speech (POS) tags and to indicate the dependencies between them. Once submited, the annotated sentence goes to a second stage of revision, where still changes can be made. After revision, the annotated sentence reaches the final stage and becomes part of the dataset.
